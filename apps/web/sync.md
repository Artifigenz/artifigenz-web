## Vision

Artifigenz is an AI-native parent company built for the agentic era. We build autonomous, agent-first products that operate at the intersection of trust, intelligence, and speed. The tagline says it all: **agents. building. agents.**

We are not a single product — we are the infrastructure layer, the holding company, the fleet commander. Every product under Artifigenz is designed to function autonomously, serve the agentic ecosystem, and ship relentlessly.

Founded by **Cooper** ([@CooperAIWorks](https://x.com/CooperAIWorks)) and **Rajan RK** ([@rajanbuilds](https://x.com/rajanbuilds)).

## What We've Built So Far

### Landing Page (artifigenz.github.io)
- **Repo:** [Artifigenz/artifigenz.github.io](https://github.com/Artifigenz/artifigenz.github.io)
- **Stack:** Single HTML file, vanilla JS, no dependencies (Google Fonts only)
- **Font:** JetBrains Mono (unified across all text — headings, body, UI)
- **Color palette:**
  - Background: `#ffffff` (white, dark mode: `#0a0a0a`)
  - Text: `#1a1a1a` (dark)
  - Text mid: `#3d3830`
  - Text dim: `#8a8078`
  - Accent: `#e85d26` (orange)
- **Logo:** Geometric recursive spiral triangle (`logo_1.png`, transparent version `logo_1-removebg-preview.png`)
- **Layout:** Hero left (brand name + tagline) / product list right ("the fleet") / founder footer
- **Particle system:** Mouse-following canvas particles — clustered formations, subtle opacity (0.03–0.12 alpha), gentle gravitational pull toward cursor
- **Animations:** Fade-up entrance on hero text and staggered product list reveal
- **Hover effects:** Product items slide right + name turns accent orange
- **Browser tab:** "Artifigenz | Agentic Era"
- **Footer:** Founded by Cooper and Rajan RK (linked to X profiles), @FigenzAI X link (single instance, footer only)
